Cognition and Brain Circuitry Laboratory
Principle Investigator: David Osher, Ph.D.

RESEARCH

Neuroimaging research continues to reveal a more consistent understanding of the functional organization of the human brain with a remarkable level of detail, yet these fundamental questions remain unanswered: What are the structural mechanisms that underlie modularity in the brain? How does knowledge of brain architecture (both structure and function) explain individual differences in behavior?

1. Does photoreceptor density influence visual processing, esp. high-level processing?

2. What is the relationship of funtional connectivity and structural connectivity? How can we fine-tune predictions of behavior based on that individual's connectivity fingerprint?
